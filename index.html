Course Details

We teach Python, a language that is great for beginners. It has a simple syntax that is great to learn, but has powerful resources for more complex projects. Google, Instagram, and Spotify all use Python to power their day-to-day services.

We will only accept six students, having a quality 1:3 teacher to student ratio. Not only this, but we will host a showcase at the end of the week so you can see what your child has learned.
