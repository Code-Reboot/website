Details

Dates

The camp will be hosted August 21 - 25, 2017. Each day will span from 1:00 - 5:00.

Age Requirements

Our camp allows all children from ages 8-11.

Daily Schedule

As many of you parents know, sitting down for a prolonged period of time can be unhealthy. During camp, we provide 2 breaks per day, including outdoor activities and playtime.

If your child needs a snack in the afternoon, please send them with one. We will be providing waterbottles as well. Please also note that we will not provide or have a set time for lunch.

Hardware Requirements

Each student will be required to bring a laptop. The laptop will need to meet the following requirements:

  • Windows or MacOS
  • 2GB Ram
  • 15GB Free Storage Space

Location

This camp is locally run in Pasadena. When you sign up, we will send you a confirmation email with the exact address.

Pricing

We think that too many programming camps are overpriced. That's why we priced Code Reboot 2017 at $100 (cash payment, due upon the first day of camp) for the week.
